The Assemblage of Parts
Chapbook (36 loose pages, bubble wrap cover)
6 x 8"
2015

   

This study of the body has little to do with the physical body—instead I thought of 'body' in terms of a collection of pre-existing work, an 'assemblage of things' that together could speak about a complex whole. I began by writing a poem of unchanged phrases culled from 10 of my academic papers, then created and collected imagery that responds to the OED's five primary definitions of 'body'. The result is a collection of unbound pages—a disembodiment.
