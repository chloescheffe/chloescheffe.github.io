Index (study)
5 newsprint broadsides
14 x 17" each
2015

   

An indexical newspaper of headlines that attempts to dull the hyperbolic voices of popular online news sources (the inclusion of The Onion, by contrast, was for my sanity). In fixing digital content to paper, I 'break' many of the headlines—they point to nothing and thus at themselves, becoming even more ridiculous. The reverse of each sheet shows one day of new headlines from each source, together forming a quantity-'quality' metric.
