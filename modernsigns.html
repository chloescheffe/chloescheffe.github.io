Modern Signs
Traffic signs on found images
2012

This signage system is a response to Charlie Chaplin's character in the 1936 film Modern Times, a good-hearted man whose attempts to function in society only disrupt it further. Traffic signs are one of the most universal systems and, much like Chaplin, mine both blend in at first glance and create disorder.
