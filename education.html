The New York Times Magazine
The Education Issue
8.9375 x 10.875"
2016

   


The 2016 Education Issue covers the state of high school in America. I created the type by manipulating the magazine's existing suite of typefaces and running them through a photocopier to get a bit of tactility.

Art direction by Matt Willey and Gail Bichler.
