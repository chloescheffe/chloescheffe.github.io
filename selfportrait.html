Untitled
Drawings on plain or laser cut paper (charcoal, areas of screenprinting and/or oil painting)
15 x 22" each
2015

   

This series is an exercise in intuitive making, as a reaction against the natural impersonality of graphic design. I love graphic design and am committed to it as a medium, but I wanted to press my capabilities apart from an imposed brief and the slick perfection of design software.
