Untitled
Drawings on plain or laser cut paper (with areas of screenprinting and/or oil painting)
15 x 22" each
2015

   

This series is an exercise in intuitive making, as a reaction against the natural impersonality of graphic design. I love graphic design and am committed to it as a medium, but I wanted to press my capabilities apart from an imposed brief and the slick perfection of design software. I utilized the physicality, imperfection, and inherent texture of silkscreen and charcoal, against the starkness of elements and processes drawn from design vernacular, to make formal decisions in pursuit of an emotive finished series.
