Museum Takeover
Logo system, posters (gloss stock, with glow-in-the-dark ink)
24 x 36" each
2014

   

Museum Takeover was an exhibition and evening of performances held at the RISD Museum after hours, with an emphasis on relational installations. My identity system highlights the night aspect, when the museum is at its least accessible and most intriguing. Classical and camp collide in the application.

The custom neon typeface is based on Knockout by Hoefler & Frere-Jones.
