Museum Takeover
Logo system, posters (gloss stock), postcards, labels
Posters 18 x 24"
2014

   

Museum Takeover was an exhibition and evening of performances held at the RISD Museum after hours, with an emphasis on relational installations. My identity system highlights the night aspect, when the museum is at its least accessible and most intriguing. Classical and camp collide in the applications.

Custom neon typeface based on Hoefler & Frere-Jones' Knockout.

RISD Programming Board
