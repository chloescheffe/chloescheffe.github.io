Traces of Absent Things
Index of Walls Behind David
9.375 x 12.75" (XX pages)

Existing photographs altered to highlight space over object. Inversion of focus, perspective, the viewer's eye, the photographer's intent. Reveal of 'invisible' space. Seeing the unseen.

Book form emphasizes space, pacing, measure, to prompt questioning of space's function.
