A Conversation Between Xu Bing and Hito Steyerl as Voiced by Chloe Scheffe and Viviane Jalil
Interview excerpt
(24 pages)
7.5 x 11"
2015

   

Grad student Viviane Jalil and I had a conversation as Hito Steyerl and Xu Bing, the goal of which was to embody the artists as best we could. We discovered a fascinating link between Bing and Steyerl's starkly different bodies of work—an interest in the falseness of images (that is, an image that has a first read and an inherent read that are opposed). This booklet, an excerpt of our conversation, expresses that falseness visually.

The complete conversation will be available soon as a full-length print-on-demand book.
