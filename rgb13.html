RISD Graduate Book 2013
Book (uncoated and coated stock, perfect bound)
5.5 x 8.5"
2013

   

The RISD Graduate Book is an annual showcasing the thesis work of graduate students in every major. The 2013 book is unique in that it's divided into two sections: one for profiles and artist statements; the other for work, project descriptions, and studio & process images. Each graduate was given a unique number (a system which also functioned as page numbers) and encouraged to seek out peers we connected them to based on data from an in-depth survey.

Conceptualized and designed with Jonathan Hanahan and Brienne Jones under the guidance of Bethany Johns. Additional members of the editorial team were Zachary Futterer, Brian James, and Aaron Tobey.
