Traces of Absent Things     Degree project, installation     2015

Traces of Absent Things is my semester-long investigation
of the index as form and language, noun and verb,
score and semiotic sign. The final project took the form of an
installation of nine works (3 objects, 6 books) and a takeaway
booklet containing an essay I wrote on the topic.
