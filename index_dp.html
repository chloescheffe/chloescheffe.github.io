Traces of Absent Things
An Installation of Nine Works
Degree project
2015

Traces of Absent Things is my semester-long investigation of the index as form and language, noun and verb, score and semiotic sign. The final project took the form of an installation of the following work and a takeaway booklet containing an essay I wrote on the topic.
