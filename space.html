'Space'
Book (smyth sewn, frayed linen tapes)
5.5 x 8.5"
2014

   

A book about digital and outer space.

To produce the written content I typed 'space' into Google, saved the first 190 images served, and pulled and alphabetized their names. Each one remains exactly as the author wrote it.


Shown at

Odds & Ends Art Book Fair, Yale University Art Gallery, 2014
