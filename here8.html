Issue 8 includes stories on hiking in Peru, music in Ghana, and food in Dublin, a portfolio of speculative passport stamps, and profiles of musician Michelle Zauner in Philadelphia and, on the cover, actor Mena Massoud in LA. Contributors include illustrators Whooli Chen and Family Brothers, photographers Chantal Anderson and Vanessa Granda, and environmental reporter Krithika Varagur.
