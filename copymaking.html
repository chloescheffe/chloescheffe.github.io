Copy-Making
Essay, booklet (12 pages, saddle stitch with loop staples; first edition of 20, second edition of 15)
5.5 x 8.5"
2014

   

Copy-Making: The Copy in Contemporary Graphic Design is a proposed exhibition I curated in response to Ellen Lupton and Andrew Blauvelt's Graphic Design: Now in Production. Copy-Making showcases contemporary designers who utilize the inherent generative nature of graphic design to create work that challenges fine art's sacrosanct original and celebrates design as a standalone discipline. The traditional notion of the copy is transformed; the throwaway byproduct becomes an exploratory multi-dimensional space. This guide to the iteration of the show installed at the RISD Museum uses my essay as a critical lens.
