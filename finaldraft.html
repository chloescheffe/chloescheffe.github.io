Final draft
Wordmark, catalogue (newsprint broadside and saddle-stitched booklet in plastic bag with screenprint; edition of 150), signage (vinyl)
Catalogue 8.5 x 11" at largest
2015

   

Final draft, the graphic design senior show, focused on process and the infinite editability of any 'finished' piece of design (all the iterations that ultimately produced it). I led the team that conceptualized and produced the catalogue and all of its components. We imagined an omniscient editor that would comment on and notate our work, and commissioned 15 seniors to produce small editioned works as insights into our collective practice.

The other designers on the catalogue team were Rachel Ossip and Stephanie Low.


The exhibition was curated and coordinated by Alice Taranto and Michael Ee. Website (not pictured) by Amrit Mazumder. Photographs of the space and opening night by Patrick Han.
