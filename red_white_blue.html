Red, White, Blue
Sticker set (9 political 'pin' stickers on 7x7" liner, high-gloss finish)
2020


Collaboration with Apply, part of an initiative to add even more visibility to voting that launched in advance of the 2020 election. The stickers nod to historic political pins, while the liner honors a number of civil rights activists who fought for the vote throughout history. Custom type inspired by the work of Sister Corita Kent.

All profits from each sticker were donated to The Voter Participation Center, a nonprofit 501(c)(3) dedicated to reshaping the American electorate to better represent the United States' changing demographics.
