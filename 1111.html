11:11
Prints on various papers
Various sizes
2014

   

Prints for a weeklong show called 11:11, which required me to make two new pieces of work daily, each completed in 11 minutes and delivered at 11am and 11pm. My focus was on subverting the natural mirroring of '11:11'. The pieces are sequential and cumulative.

11:11 was conceptualized by Elie Andersen. The other artists were Alice Taranto, Lukas Eigler-Harding, Elizabeth Goodspeed, Amrit Mazumder, Mina Park, Will Urmston, Ben Adman, Drea Sullivan, and Oscar Robert.
