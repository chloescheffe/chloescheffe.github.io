Be Water
Poster (30x40", tiled; original drawing pen & ink, pencil)
2020

Poster for Post-Contact, an installation project in Providence, Rhode Island curated by Jack Fahnestock. This design is a response to Black Lives Matter. It expresses the hope I sense in the current moment, in the movement, in our nation recognizing its evils and crying out against them. I believe in the power of protest; I also believe in peace. “Be water” is a call to be fluid. Be willing to change, then change. Be reflective. Be forgiving, but remain strong. Get angry when you must. Use force when you must.
