Box-Triangle-Border
Score, process booklet (12 pages with gatefold, saddle stitched)
8.5 x 11" at largest
2014

   

Box-Triangle-Border is a score written to produce abstract images as the residue of a designed interaction between players. The process book includes the development and refinement of the rules through visual proofs, the finished rules themselves, stills from a video, and the pieces produced with the final score.
