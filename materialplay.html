Material Play, Vol. 1
Book (48 pages, saddle stitched, with foil stamp), hexagonal box (collapsable, with foil stamp)
9 x 12"
2014

   

A study of the materiality of language (its content) and design (its form) as an index of famous literary characters.

I produced all of the textures on a scanner bed. The hexagonal box flattens when the book is removed.


Shown at

Senior Invitational, Woods-Gerry Gallery, 2015

Final draft, RISD Graphic Design Senior Show, Woods-Gerry Gallery, 2015

Odds & Ends Art Book Fair, Yale University Art Gallery, 2014
